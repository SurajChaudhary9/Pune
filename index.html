About Pune

Pune, also known as the "Oxford of the East," is a vibrant and diverse city located in the western state of Maharashtra, India. With a rich historical heritage, a thriving modern culture, and breathtaking natural beauty, Pune offers a delightful experience to its residents and visitors alike.

Home to prestigious educational institutions, cutting-edge technology hubs, and a thriving arts scene, Pune is a city that celebrates both tradition and progress. Its cosmopolitan atmosphere and warm hospitality make it a melting pot of cultures, welcoming people from all walks of life.

Explore the historical landmarks, such as the grand Aga Khan Palace, Shaniwar Wada, and the iconic Saras Baug, which will transport you back in time. Discover the vibrant markets and bustling streets, where you can indulge in delectable street food and shop for intricate handicrafts made by skilled artisans.

For nature enthusiasts, Pune offers a myriad of opportunities to connect with nature. From serene hill stations like Lonavala and Khandala to picturesque lakes like Pashan Lake and Khadakwasla, nature's beauty surrounds the city, providing a serene escape from the urban hustle.

Capture breathtaking sunsets over Pune city.

Whether you are here to explore the city's history, savor its culinary delights, soak in the arts and culture, or simply relax amidst nature's wonders, Pune promises an unforgettable experience that will stay with you forever. So, come, immerse yourself in the charm of Pune and create memories that will be cherished for a lifetime!
